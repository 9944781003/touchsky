Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which TouchSky Technologies collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the www.TouchSkytechnologies.com website ("Site"). We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our Site.
